About Us

AWPO with its Headquarters at Delhi has a PAN India network of 16 Placement Nodes at major industrial hubs and 50 Placement Cells at Regimental Centres / Area, Sub Area Headquarters. AWPO provides assistance to ESM to enable a seamless transition from service to civilian life.

Apart from providing jobs in Corporate Sector, AWPO also assists in preparation for Govt jobs and skilling through Recognition of Prior Learning programme..
